Dieter Rams: 10 Principles
for Good Design

1
Good design is inventive

Good design is made for it's current situation and circumstance, it's priority should be the present. However, it can always be improved or altered.

2
Good design must have a purpose

Design must be functional in order to be designed well. There is an emphasis on usefulness in order for design to be worth anything.

3
Good design is beautiful

As beauty is a commonality of everyday life, good design must also exemplify beauty. Aesthetic must be taken in to consideration when design is involved. Well-made design is beautiful.

4
Good design is self-explanatory

A person should not have a difficult time understanding a design. One should be able to read and understand good design just by looking at it.

5
Good design is not overly extravagant

Well-made design is not intended to be art or d√©cor, it exists for more than just aestheticism. Aesthetic and self-expression should not hinder a product's use or purpose.

6
Good design is straightforward

A design should not promise anything it can't do. It is upfront about what it can and cannot do.

7
Good design is long-lasting

Good design isn't meant to be "fashionable", it is not meant to last as long as a trend or fad.

8
Good design is thorough and intentional

Nothing is left to chance in design. Everything is done with intention to help the user.

9
Good design does not harm the environment

There should not be an excess of resources used in good design, nor should a design be wasteful. Good design reduces resources used and any pollution produced. It does not use an extensive amount of resources.

10
Good design is minimal

Good design is not "burdened" with what is unnecessary. Less is more.
